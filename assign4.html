We have a very successful certificate program.

A certificate shows you have applied yourself in a specific business area, and have completed a series of courses, which makes you more successful in the workplace. A Certificate of Completion requires three or more courses, and a Certificate of Achievement requires 18 or more units. You can combine a Certificate of Achievement with General Education courses and earn your Associate in Arts degree.
